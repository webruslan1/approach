Approach

A crucial feature of our approach is the perception of the client's business as our own.

We do not just execute IT development according to the technical requirements - since our team not only has programmers but also experienced marketing specialists, product, and analytics managers. We are ready to understand all the needs fully, delve into each client's business, and offer a solution as profitable as possible in terms of cost and timing.

Our developers are always on the "crest of the wave." We adhere to the policy of using only the most effective IT methodologies and modern technologies.

The result —
what is important

Each of our employees feels personal responsibility for the result of his actions - and the final result, in the first place, we consider not the satisfaction of our client, but the user experience that the customers of our client receive from using the solutions developed by us.

Therefore, in development, we abide by the principle of triple control - any solution passes three stages of testing before it reaches the customer and production:

  • The developer himself

    Each developer necessarily performs automatic tests for any element of the application that he creates

  • The testing department

    Testers verify the application for compliance with the technical specifications and the correct operation of all implemented functionality

  • Employees of other departments

    We believe that the most effective testing is by third-party users, as they can see and point out all the obscure parts and difficulties during their first-time experience
